About Kuznets Curve

An insight into economic inequality and development

What is Kuznets Curve?

The Kuznets Curve is a graphical representation of the hypothesis that as an economy develops, market forces first increase and then decrease economic inequality.

Visualization

Key Facts

  • Proposed by Simon Kuznets in 1955.
  • Suggests an inverted U-shaped relationship between inequality and income per capita.
  • Often debated in modern economics.
